About us

The sole purpose of this site is to relay the talent of the Nordic Music Industry's best female artists of the past few decades to the rest of the world. You may or may not have heard of them, but we feel its time to lift the dome and hear the music properly. The page is continuously growing so please bookmark us and visit often. Our Social Media is also very active!

Do you know of an Artist we should feature? Please let us know.. After all, it's music to our ears!
